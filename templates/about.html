About Medical Advisor

Our Mission

Medical Advisor aims to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare by providing an accessible first step in understanding potential health conditions.

Our AI-powered system analyzes symptoms described in natural language and matches them to potential medical conditions, helping users understand what might be going on and when to seek professional medical attention.

Technology

Medical Advisor combines:

  • Advanced NLP - Understands symptoms described in everyday language
  • Medical Knowledge Base - Contains 377+ symptoms and 760+ conditions
  • Machine Learning - Trained on extensive medical datasets
  • AI Assistance - Powered by Google's Gemini models

How It Works

  1. You describe your symptoms in natural language
  2. Our AI maps your description to precise medical symptoms
  3. The system analyzes potential conditions based on symptom patterns
  4. You receive a list of possible conditions with confidence levels
  5. Important: You're advised to consult a healthcare professional

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • No personal health information is stored after your session ends
  • Conversations are processed securely and never shared
  • We comply with healthcare data protection standards
  • No medical records are created or maintained
Important: Medical Advisor does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider.
